About Santa Ana Calm Waters Academy (CIA) - Leading CBE School in Ruiru

Located in Ruiru Town near Ruiru Law Courts, our school emphasizes on Christian values, discipline, and academic excellence.

Founded in 2017, our mission is to nurture holistic development, character, and academic excellence.

We create a nurturing environment where students thrive academically, socially, and spiritually.

Our Competency-Based Education (CBE) from Reception to Grade 9 builds knowledge and values.

Spacious classrooms, serene location, and a team of dedicated educators.

We focus on integrity, discipline, and compassion to raise responsible leaders.

Strong collaboration with parents and the community for student success.

We are proud of our achievements and positive impact on student lives.
