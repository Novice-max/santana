### 1. TIMING FOR REVIEW REQUESTS

#### Best Times to Ask for Reviews:
- **After successful school events** (cultural day, sports day, graduation)
- **End of term/semester** when parents are satisfied with progress
- **After resolving any concerns** - turn negative experiences into positive ones
- **During parent-teacher conferences** when engagement is high
- **After new student orientation** when first impressions are positive

#### Avoid Requesting During:
- Academic struggles or disciplinary issues
- School holidays when engagement is low  
- Crisis situations or negative incidents
- Exam periods when stress is high

## üõ°Ô∏è CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### 1. CRISIS RESPONSE PROTOCOL

#### Level 1: Minor Concerns (3-star reviews, small complaints)
- **Response Time:** Within 4 hours
- **Action:** Direct, empathetic response + offer to discuss offline
- **Follow-up:** Phone call or meeting within 24 hours

#### Level 2: Significant Issues (1-2 star reviews, serious complaints)
- **Response Time:** Within 2 hours
- **Action:** Senior management response + immediate offline discussion
- **Follow-up:** Formal meeting within 12 hours, written action plan

#### Level 3: Public Crisis (viral negative content, serious incidents)
- **Response Time:** Within 1 hour
- **Action:** Director/Manager personal response + crisis communication plan
- **Follow-up:** Public transparency, corrective actions, community meeting

### 2. CRISIS RESPONSE TEMPLATES

#### For Service Issues:
"We sincerely apologize for this experience. This doesn't reflect our values at Santa Ana Calm Waters Academy. We're taking immediate action to address this issue and prevent future occurrences. Please contact Director Mr. Anthony Maina directly at +254 742 935 110. We're committed to making this right."

#### For Communication Issues:
"Thank you for this feedback. Clear communication is essential in education, and we clearly fell short here. We're implementing new communication protocols to ensure all parents feel heard and informed. Please let us discuss how we can improve moving forward."

#### For Academic Concerns:
"Every student's academic success is our priority. We take this concern very seriously and would like to review your child's specific situation with you immediately. Our academic team is ready to develop an action plan to address these challenges."

## üéØ SUCCESS METRICS

### Target Goals:
- **Review Volume:** 5-10 new reviews per month
- **Average Rating:** Maintain 4.5+ stars across platforms
- **Response Rate:** 100% response to all reviews within 24 hours
- **Sentiment:** 80%+ positive review sentiment
- **Conversion:** 1 in 3 website visitors from reviews convert to inquiries

### Red Flags to Monitor:
- Rating dropping below 4.0 stars
- Multiple negative reviews in short period
- Consistent complaints about same issues
- Competitor ratings significantly higher
- Lack of recent reviews (review drought)

**Remember:** Reputation building is a marathon, not a sprint. Consistent, authentic engagement and genuine commitment to improvement will yield the best long-term results!
