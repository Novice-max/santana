Student Welfare & Support Services

At Santa Ana Calm Waters Academy, we prioritize the holistic well-being of every student. Our comprehensive welfare programs ensure that each child feels safe, supported, and valued throughout their educational journey with us.

Parent Partnership in Student Welfare

We believe that student welfare is a collaborative effort between school and home. Our parent partnership programs include:

  • Regular Communication: Updates on student progress and well-being
  • Welfare Meetings: Discussions about individual student needs
  • Home-School Coordination: Consistent support strategies
  • Emergency Contacts: 24/7 communication for urgent matters
  • Feedback Systems: Parent input on welfare programs
