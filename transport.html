School Transport Services

Santa Ana Calm Waters Academy provides safe, reliable, and comfortable transportation services for our students. Our transport system is designed to ensure that every student reaches school and returns home safely and on time.

Our Transport Features

Safety First

  • Professional, licensed drivers
  • Regular vehicle maintenance
  • Safety protocols and drills
  • First aid kits in all vehicles

Timely Service

  • Punctual pick-up and drop-off
  • Efficient route planning
  • Real-time updates
  • Flexible scheduling

Route Coverage

  • Extensive area coverage
  • Strategic pick-up points
  • Door-to-door service
  • Route optimization

Staff

  • Trained drivers
  • Vehicle attendants
  • Transport coordinators
  • Support staff
